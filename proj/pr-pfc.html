Edges, Satellites, Stations

Based on a multi-scalar and cross-sectional approach, “Edges, Satellites, Stations” seeks to promote sustainable growth in one of the most deprived areas of Barcelona. By starting out with analysing the evolution of basic socio-economic and urban key indicators, the need to propose a plan that enables a dynamic and inclusive transformation for the area and its inhabitants becomes apparent.

1st Prize Award UNESCO-UIA & MIDO Student Design Prize for Responsible Architecture, 2017

Developed by Alba Alsina Maqueda, 2015
