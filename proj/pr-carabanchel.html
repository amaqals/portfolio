Creative Carabanchel

Carabanchel Creativa is the pilot project of the urban activation model of the IED Innovation Lab, the innovation center of the Istituto Europeo di Design in Madrid. A process of social innovation, activation and connection between citizens, creative economics, institutions and associations, which incorporates visualization technologies VR so that neighbors are those who project their city, and can experience the future before it is reality.
Collaboration in the first step of the project: database generation and visualization of the characteristic locations and agents present in the neighbourhood: industrial heritage ( detection of buildings of industrial character), traditional commerce (activities of the professional sectors with historical presence in the district) and strategic allies (neighborhood associations, indirectly related agents with the Orange Economy and other groups and organizations located in the district).

Project coordinator: IED City Lab, IED Innovation Lab Madrid.
